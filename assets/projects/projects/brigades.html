Global Engineering Brigades — Sustainable Water System (In Progress)

Status: Ongoing — Spring 2026

Overview

As part of Boston University’s Global Engineering Brigades, I am working on a multidisciplinary team to design and implement a sustainable clean water system for communities in Guatemala with limited access to safe drinking water.

My role

  • Supporting requirements gathering and constraint analysis (cost, materials, maintenance)
  • Assisting in system design (filtration, storage, and distribution)
  • Planning testing and validation for water quality and system performance

Skills & tools

  • Engineering design and systems thinking
  • Team-based problem solving
  • Impact-driven project planning

Why this matters

Access to clean water is a foundational public health issue. This project aims to deliver a solution that is not only technically sound, but also locally maintainable and culturally appropriate for long-term sustainability.

Next milestones

  • Finalize system design
  • Field installation in Guatemala
  • Post-installation testing and iteration
